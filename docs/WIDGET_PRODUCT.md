# Widget-first Familiar

The Home Screen widget is the primary retention surface, not a miniature copy of the app.

## Comparable companion loop

Familiar borrows the useful loop common to brain-pet and focus-companion products: capture something, do a small meaningful action, and watch a companion grow. The implementation remains guilt-free—no death, runaway pet, broken streak, or escalating need state.

- Capture a thought or optional daily intention in the app.
- Start focus from the widget or app.
- Pet/check in directly from the widget.
- Earn experience from completed focus, reflections, and optional tasks.
- Unlock world-specific poses, decor, and evolutionary details.
- Review a Journey timeline rather than a punitive streak calendar.

## System surfaces

- Small widget: pet pose, name, level, Pet and Focus actions.
- Medium widget: adds mood, bond, and daily focus minutes.
- Lock Screen widget: pet silhouette and focus status (next target).
- Live Activity: accurate active-focus countdown, pause/end actions, and Dynamic Island presentation (next target).

WidgetKit controls scheduling. Timeline poses should create a sense of life without promising continuous animation. Meaningful writes go through the App Group snapshot and request a timeline reload. The app owns canonical progression and schema migration.
